Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

1. Data Processing

All text conversion and font generation happens entirely in your browser. Your text is never sent to our servers for the font conversion process. The conversion is performed using JavaScript Unicode mapping that runs locally on your device.

2. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to store:

  • Font combo preferences (fg_saved_combos)
  • Input history (fg_history)
  • Login session data

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync (Pro feature).

3. Google Sign-In

We use Google Sign-In solely for Pro membership verification. We only collect your email address and display name for account purposes. We do not access any other Google account data.

4. No Text Collection

We do not collect, store, or analyze any text you input into our font generator. Your input text and generated fonts remain entirely on your device.

5. Cookies

We use minimal cookies only for Google Sign-In authentication. No tracking cookies or third-party analytics are used.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare Pages & Workers — Hosting and API
  • Google Identity Services — User authentication

7. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our website.
